About MindPath

Our Purpose

MindPath was created to provide teenagers and young adults in the United Kingdom with clear, accessible information about mental health and how to access help and support.

Why We Created MindPath

We recognise that:

  • Many young people experience mental health difficulties but don't know where to turn for help
  • There is often confusion about what mental health is
  • Stigma can prevent young people from seeking support
  • Young people need access to reliable, trustworthy information
  • Many young people feel alone in their struggles

Our Goals

  1. Provide clear information about mental health
  2. Help young people recognise mental health difficulties early
  3. Provide practical self-help strategies
  4. Signpost young people to professional support and services
  5. Reduce stigma around mental health
  6. Support parents and educators

Our Values

  • Youth-centred - we focus on young people's needs
  • Trustworthy - we provide accurate information
  • Accessible - we use clear, simple language
  • Non-judgemental - we don't judge or discriminate
  • Supportive - we aim to support and empower
  • Inclusive - we welcome everyone

Disclaimer

MindPath provides information and guidance about mental health. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a healthcare professional or emergency services immediately.

Sources

The information on this website is based on guidance from the NHS, Mind, YoungMinds, and other reputable mental health organisations.
