About MindPath

MindPath is a project aimed at providing young people in the UK with clear, supportive information about mental health and ways to access help.

Our goals are to:

  • Raise awareness of mental health issues.
  • Reduce stigma around seeking help.
  • Provide links to reliable UK services.

Mental health awareness is essential for healthy communities. By educating and supporting young people we hope to make a positive difference.
