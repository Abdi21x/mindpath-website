Self Help Tips

Managing stress

  • Take regular breaks
  • Exercise regularly
  • Talk to someone you trust

Improving sleep

  1. Maintain a bedtime routine.
  2. Avoid screens before bed.
  3. Create a calm environment.

Healthy routines

Eat balanced meals, stay hydrated, and plan your day with realistic goals.

Mindfulness

Practice breathing exercises and focus on the present moment to reduce anxiety.
