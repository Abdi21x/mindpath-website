Self-Help Tips

Managing Stress

  1. Identify what causes your stress
  2. Take breaks when you feel overwhelmed
  3. Practice deep breathing
  4. Limit caffeine and sugar
  5. Talk to someone you trust
  6. Set realistic goals

Improving Your Sleep

  1. Keep a consistent sleep schedule
  2. Make your bedroom dark and quiet
  3. Avoid screens before bed
  4. Avoid caffeine and heavy meals before bed
  5. Develop a bedtime routine
  6. Exercise during the day

Healthy Routines

  1. Eat a balanced diet
  2. Exercise regularly
  3. Spend time in nature
  4. Do activities you enjoy
  5. Spend time with friends and family
  6. Limit social media use

Mindfulness and Relaxation

  1. Practice meditation
  2. Try muscle relaxation exercises
  3. Imagine a peaceful place
  4. Try yoga or stretching
  5. Do creative activities
  6. Spend time outdoors
