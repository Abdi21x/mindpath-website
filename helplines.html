UK Mental Health Helplines

If you need immediate support, here are helplines you can contact:

Samaritans

Phone: 116 123 (available 24/7)
Email: jo@samaritans.org
Confidential emotional support for people in crisis.

Childline

Phone: 0800 1111 (available 24/7)
Website: www.childline.org.uk
Free, confidential support for children and young people under 19.

Mind Infoline

Phone: 0300 123 3393 (Monday to Friday 9am to 6pm)
Text: Text HELLO to 50808
Information and support about mental health conditions.

YoungMinds Crisis Messenger

Text: YM to 85258
Text support for young people in crisis.

CALM

Phone: 0800 585 858 (5pm to midnight)
Support for men's mental health.

When to Call 999

Call 999 if you or someone else is in immediate danger, having thoughts of harming yourself, or having a severe mental health emergency.
