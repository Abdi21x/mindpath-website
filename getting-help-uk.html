Getting Help in the UK

You can access support in many different ways depending on your needs and location.

GP Support

Your GP can listen, offer advice and refer you to specialist mental health services.

School or University Counselling

Most educational institutions provide free counselling for students.

NHS Services

The NHS website lists local mental health teams and crisis services. Visit www.nhs.uk.
