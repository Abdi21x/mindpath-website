MindPath – Mental Health Support for Young People

We provide information, guidance and links to help teenagers and young adults in the UK look after their mental health.

What is mental health?

Mental health relates to our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel and act. Everyone has mental health just like everyone has physical health.

Why it matters

Strong mental health helps us cope with stress, build relationships and make decisions. Early support can make a big difference.
