Frequently Asked Questions

Is what I say confidential?

Yes, most services are confidential unless there is a risk of harm to yourself or others.

I'm scared of being judged.

Professionals are trained to be non-judgmental. It's okay to feel nervous – many people feel the same.

Does it cost anything?

Many services, especially through the NHS or schools, are free. Some charities may ask for donations but they aim to keep support accessible.

Where can I get help?

Start with your GP, a trusted adult, or by contacting a helpline listed on our Helplines page.
