Common Mental Health Challenges

Anxiety

Anxiety is a feeling of worry or nervousness about things that might happen. It is normal to feel anxious sometimes, but when it becomes overwhelming, it can affect daily life.

Symptoms include racing thoughts, difficulty concentrating, and physical symptoms like chest tightness.

Depression

Depression is more than just feeling sad. It is a condition that affects how a person thinks, feels, and manages daily life.

Symptoms include persistent sadness, loss of interest in activities, sleep problems, and low energy.

Stress

Stress is the body's response to pressure or demands. Some stress is normal, but too much stress can be harmful.

Common stress triggers for young people include school work, relationships, family problems, and future worries.

Burnout

Burnout is exhaustion caused by prolonged stress. It happens when someone is overworked or lacks support.

Signs include extreme tiredness, feeling detached, reduced productivity, and loss of motivation.

Getting Help

If you are struggling with any of these challenges, please reach out to someone you trust. Talk to a friend, family member, teacher, or counselor.
