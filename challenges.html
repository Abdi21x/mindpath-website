Common Challenges

Anxiety

A feeling of worry or fear that can be overwhelming. It's common among young people.

Depression

Persistent sadness and loss of interest. Can affect sleep, energy and concentration.

Stress

Response to pressure from school, work or relationships. Short-term stress is normal but long-term stress can be harmful.

Burnout

Feeling exhausted and unable to cope after prolonged stress. Often linked to overwork or high expectations.
