What is mental health?

Mental health describes how we think, feel and act. It includes our emotional and psychological state and affects every aspect of our lives.

Why is it important?

Good mental health helps you cope with stress, relate to others and make healthy choices. It is important at every stage of life.

Key points

  1. Mental health changes over time.
  2. Everyone has it, just like physical health.
  3. Support and understanding can improve outcomes.
  • Mental health affects learning, work and relationships.
  • There are many ways to look after it.
  • Stigma can prevent people seeking help.
