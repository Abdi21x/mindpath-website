What is Mental Health?

Definition

Mental health relates to our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and behave.

Why is Mental Health Important?

Good mental health helps us:

  • Manage stress and challenges
  • Build healthy relationships
  • Work towards our goals
  • Enjoy life and activities
  • Handle difficult emotions

Mental Health is a Spectrum

Everyone experiences different levels of mental health at different times. Some days are better than others, and that is normal.

Common Myths

Myth: Mental health problems are a sign of weakness.

Fact: Mental health problems are common and can happen to anyone. Getting help shows strength.

Myth: Young people don't have mental health problems.

Fact: Many young people experience mental health challenges. Early support helps.
