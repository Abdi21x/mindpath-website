For Parents and Educators

Supporting Young People with Mental Health Difficulties

As a parent or educator, you play an important role in supporting young people's mental health.

Recognising When a Young Person is Struggling

Look out for changes in:

  • Mood or behaviour
  • Sleep or appetite
  • School or work performance
  • Interest in activities they usually enjoy
  • Relationships and friendships
  • Confidence and self-esteem

How to Help

  • Listen without judgment
  • Create a safe space for them to talk
  • Take their feelings seriously
  • Ask how they are really doing
  • Encourage professional help when needed
  • Look after your own mental health too

School-Based Support

Schools can support young people through:

  • School counselling services
  • Pastoral care staff
  • Mental health education
  • Links with external services

In an Emergency

If a young person is in crisis or at risk of harming themselves, contact 999, your local A&E, or a crisis service.
